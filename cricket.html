Rules and Regulation for cricket


Rules and Regulations:-
1. The Match will be played in willson Ball
2. The Match will be played 6 Over's per Innings.
3. Umpire decision will be final decision.
4. Team should have 11 Main players and 3 substitutes
5. College ID card is mandatory for every player.
6. The winners and Runners will be awarded with Trophy,
Certificate and Cash Prize.
7. Special Prizes will be awarded for Best Bats Man, Best Bowler,
and Best Player of the Tournament.
8. Refreshments will be provided for the players.
9. The Entry Fee will be 3000/- Rupees.
10.Last Minute Entry will be not entertained.
11.A player participated in one team cannot participate in another team
12.Undisciplined team/player will be disqualified
13.Rolling sub is not allowed
14.Each innings will be played with new ball
15.Only Coach/Pt teacher will be allowed to talk with the umpire
16.Only bowling action is allowed.
