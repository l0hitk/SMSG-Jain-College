Rules and Regulation for Dancing


RULES FOR SOLO AND GROUP DANCING:-
1.The song should be at least 3 to 4 minutes .
2.Participants for group- 4 to 6 minimum, maximum 8
3.Judging Criteria
i.Coverage of the stage
ii.choreography
iii.song selection
iv.costumes
v.creativity
vi.expression
vii.coordination
viii. presentation , will be noticed.
4.Song should be sent while registering or at least 1 hour before the schedule
5.Any type of dance can be done ,Usage of props and production material is allowed ,these have to be taken care by yourself.
6.Participants should be present before 30 minutes.
7.Teams performing against the rules and regulations will be disqualified.
