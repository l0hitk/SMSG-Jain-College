Rules and Regulation for VOLLEY BALL


RULES FOR VOLLEY BALL:-
1. Each team consist of 6 players and 3 substitutes.
2. The match will be played on the basis of TT points( 3 sets)
3. Referee decision will be final decision.
4. Only the captain and coach can talk to the Referee
5. The match will be knock-out basis.
6. The Winner and Runners will be awarded trophy and certificate.
7. The entry fee is 1000/- Rs. only.
8. The last date for entry fee is 30th august
9.Rolling sub is not allowed
