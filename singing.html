Rules and Regulation for SINGING


RULES FOR SOLO AND GROUP SINGING:-
1.Time allotted for each performance is 2-3 minutes.
2.Participants will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while singing and are free to use karaoke
3. The participants will be judged on the song selection, voice quality, clarity, rhythm and their appeal to the audience.
4.The participants should bring their own musical instruments if required in the performance.
5.Choice of song is open to the participants but the song should not have any slang or derogatory language.
6.Participants should be present before 30 minutes.
7.Teams performing against the rules and regulations will be disqualified
8.Winners will be given a Medal and a certificate.
