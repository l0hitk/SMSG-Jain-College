Rules and Regulation for Techie Delight


RULES FOR TECHIE DELIGHT:-
1. Timings [1 hour ]
2. Participants should be present before 30 minutes of competition.
3. Programming Languages C & C++.
4. A team must contain only 2 members.
5. Participants Should not carry any electrical gadgets [Pen drive, phone, air pods, smart watch]
6. No communication with others during the execution of program.
7. Should not carry any books or slips.
8. Shoes are not allowed in the lab.
9. Prize will be given to the person who executes the program first with least errors.
10. Winners will be awarded a certificate and medal, Judges decision will be final
11. Teams against the rules and regulations will be disqualified.
