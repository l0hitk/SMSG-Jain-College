Rules and Regulation for Treasure Hunt


TREASURE HUNT RULES:-
1.Five participants in a team
2.Time limit is one hour.
3.There will be given a list of objects,hints,clues to find the given treasure.
4.Teams must stay together during the treasure hunt and are encouraged to work together to slove the clues.
5.The team who find all the clues before the other teams will be declared as the winner.
6.Participants should be present before 30 minutes.
7.Teams performing against the rules and regulations will be disqualified.
