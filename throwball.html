Rules and Regulation for Throwball


RULES FOR Throwball:-
1.Throw ball is a team sport. Each team consists of twelve players with 7 active players at the time of play and 5 substitutes.
2.The entry fee will be 1000rs/- only
3. The match is played for best of 3 sets for 15 points in each set with TT points
4. The rules will be based on the new guidelines issued by the karnataka throwball federation .
5. The service ball should not touch the net.
6. Double touches are not allowed for receiving the service ball.
7. The players should serve the ball after the whistle is blown and within 5 seconds.
8.The match will be on knock-out basis.
9.The Winner and Runners will be awarded trophy and certificate.
